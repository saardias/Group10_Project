Cars War instructions:
Each turn every player pick a card from the top of the deck.
the player with the higher card is the winning of the current turn.
the winner of the turn gets points according to the values of the cards in the fileds.

the premium player can change the his current card with the top card of the deck. he can do it 3 times in a game.

the winner of the game is the player with the higer number of points.
